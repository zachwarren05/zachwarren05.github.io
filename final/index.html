Our Purpose

Welcome to the Chico Chamber of Commerce!
Our Chamber works hard to give back to the community that has given us so much. We communicate and work with all members of the community to create a more unified environment. We also want to make sure that small businesses are heard within the community and are able to voice their opinions in the city of Chico. The Chamber's main goal is to support local businesses and grow our local community.
Become a part of the voice! We welcome you to take a seat with us in the Chamber so that you have the opportunity to have your voice heard. Your business deserves the same amount of attention as everyone else.
